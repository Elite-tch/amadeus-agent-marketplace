# Amadeus Wallet Guide

Learn how to set up and use the Amadeus Wallet for the marketplace.

## Installing the Wallet

### Chrome/Edge/Brave

1. Visit the [Amadeus Wallet Extension](https://chromewebstore.google.com/detail/amadeus-wallet/gigmkdnbhopbandngplohmilogilbkjn)
2. Click **Add to Browser**
3. Pin the extension to your toolbar for easy access

### Creating a New Wallet

1. Click the Amadeus Wallet icon
2. Select **Create New Wallet**
3. Set a strong password
4. **Write down your seed phrase** (12-24 words)
5. Confirm your seed phrase
6. Your wallet is ready!

> **Warning**: Never share your seed phrase with anyone. It gives full access to your funds.

### Importing an Existing Wallet

1. Click **Import Wallet**
2. Enter your seed phrase
3. Set a password
4. Click **Import**

## Understanding Your Wallet

### Wallet Address

Your wallet address is a unique identifier starting with letters and numbers (e.g., `5Kd3N7vW...`). This is what you'll use to:
- Receive AMA tokens
- Identify yourself on the blockchain
- Publish agents as a creator

### Balance Display

The wallet shows your balance in **AMA tokens**.

- **1 AMA** = 1,000,000,000 atomic units (9 decimals)
- Balances update automatically after transactions

## Getting AMA Tokens

### Testnet Tokens (Free)

For testing the marketplace:
1. Copy your wallet address
2. Visit the Amadeus Faucet
3. Paste your address
4. Request tokens
5. Wait for confirmation (~10 seconds)

### Mainnet Tokens

For real transactions:
1. Purchase from exchanges
2. Withdraw to your wallet address
3. Allow 1-2 minutes for confirmation

## Connecting to the Marketplace

1. Visit the marketplace
2. Click **Connect Wallet**
3. Approve the connection request
4. Your address will appear in the navbar

### Disconnecting

1. Click your wallet address in the navbar
2. Select **Disconnect**

## Making Transactions

### Approving Purchases

When you buy an agent:
1. A transaction preview appears
2. Click **Confirm Purchase**
3. The wallet popup opens
4. Review the details
5. Click **Sign Transaction**
6. Wait for confirmation

### Transaction Details

Each transaction shows:
- **Recipient**: Agent creator's address
- **Amount**: Price in AMA
- **Description**: What the payment is for
- **Fee**: Network transaction fee

### Viewing Transaction History

1. Click the wallet icon
2. Go to **Activity**
3. Click any transaction to view details
4. Click the transaction hash to view on the explorer

## Security Best Practices

### Do's ✅
- Store your seed phrase offline
- Use a strong password
- Lock your wallet when not in use
- Verify recipient addresses before sending
- Keep your browser and extension updated

### Don'ts ❌
- Never share your seed phrase
- Don't store seed phrases digitally
- Don't approve suspicious transactions
- Never send tokens to unknown addresses
- Don't use public/shared computers for wallet access

## Troubleshooting

### Wallet Not Detected
- Refresh the page
- Ensure the extension is enabled
- Try disconnecting and reconnecting
- Clear browser cache

### Transaction Failed
- Check your balance
- Ensure sufficient gas fees
- Try again in a few moments
- Increase gas limit if needed

### Balance Not Updating
- Wait 30 seconds for blockchain sync
- Refresh the page
- Check transaction status on explorer

## Recovery

If you lose access to your wallet:
1. Install the Amadeus Wallet extension
2. Click **Import Wallet**
3. Enter your seed phrase
4. Set a new password
5. Your funds will be restored

> **Critical**: Without your seed phrase, funds are **permanently** lost!
